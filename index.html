About this planner

What is this?
This is a free, private tool that helps trans and non-binary people in the UK find out what legal steps they need to take to change their name and gender marker on official documents.

Who is it for?
Anyone living in the UK over the age of 16 who wants to update their legal documents to match their gender identity. This includes people who only want a name change, only a gender marker change, or both. There is also guidance for people from outside the UK who hold a visa or eVisa (a digital visa stored online rather than as a physical sticker). This planner is designed for adults and young people aged 16 and over, as the legal processes for those under 16 require parental consent and follow different steps.

How does it work?
You answer a short set of questions about your situation: what documents you already have, what you have already updated, and what you want to do. The planner then creates a personal step-by-step action plan for you, with clear instructions and links to official resources.

Step-by-step or checklist?
Both options produce the same plan. The step-by-step wizard shows one question at a time. This is good if you are not sure where to start. The checklist shows all questions at once. This is good if you already know your situation well. You can switch between them at any time.

Is my information safe?
Yes. Nothing you enter is ever sent to a server or stored outside your own device. Your progress is saved in your browser so you can return to it later. Press the red ⚠️ button or the Esc key at any time to instantly leave the page.

Is this legal, medical, or financial advice?
No. This planner gives general guidance only. It helps you understand the steps and paperwork needed to update your legal documents. It is not a replacement for advice from a qualified professional. Always check important steps on official GOV.UK pages before submitting any applications.

📖 Usage guide

Accessibility: This page has full support for users with accessible needs or devices, as well as screen readers. You can also use tab and arrow keys to move through elements on the page.

Red '⚠️' button: Quick exit. Make a quick exit at any time by clicking this button, or press Esc on your keyboard (on most browsers), to instantly close this page and go to a different website. This also removes this page from your "Back" button history.

'?' button: About this planner. Opens a box with answers to common questions, such as "What is this?", "Who is it for?", and "How does it work?"

'☀️' or '🌙' button: Change theme. This button switches the page between a light and dark appearance. By default, the page will use your device's setting.

Get started: Click either "Answer step-by-step questions" or "Fill out a single checklist" to get started. You can switch between either mode at any time and your progress will be the same.

'↹' button: This appears once you have started answering questions until you have an action plan. It will switch between answering step-by-step questions and a checklist.

Action plan: Once you've gone through the questions, you have an action plan. This screen shows you a list of actions grouped by the order to tackle them in. It will also show you the approximate difficulty and cost of completing the action. Each item can also be checked off or marked as unneeded using the buttons on the left of each item on your action plan. Your action plan is saved to your device, and you can resume at any time by reopening this page where you will be prompted to continue or start a new plan. If the planner is updated, you may see a screen prompting you to review your answers.

'↺' button: New plan. This deletes your answers, your action plan, and all tracked progress, taking you back to the start screen. You need to confirm by clicking a second time. This cannot be undone.

'✏️' button: To make different choices responding to questions, click this button. You can switch between views if you prefer to answer questions step-by-step or using the checklist and once you have made changes, click "Update my action plan" to view your new action plan.

'👁️' button: Focus mode. Clicking this button will hide items and sections on your action planned you've marked as done or not needed, making the view much more compact allowing you to focus on what items are left.

'🔗' button: Share. Click to copy a link to your action plan. You can use it to return to your action plan on this device or other devices, or to share with people you fully trust. Be careful: this link includes sensitive information such as your immigration status, employment situation, and GRC intentions.

'🖨️' button: Print or save to PDF. This will open the print dialog in your web browser. It will allow you to either print your action plan or save it to your device. It will show any progress you have already tracked and expand the 'More information' sections.

'⟲' button: Reset progress. This resets all the progress checkboxes on your action plan back to "not started", but keeps your plan and answers intact. You need to confirm by clicking a second time.

🔒 Privacy policy

Data storage: This planner is designed to protect your privacy.
No personal information is collected, and no answers are ever sent to a server. The website runs entirely inside your own web browser.

To let you tick off tasks and return later, your progress is saved on the device you are using. You can delete all saved data at any time by clicking "New plan" or by clearing your browser history.

A security cookie may be set by Cloudflare, the hosting provider, to protect the site from automated attacks. This is not used for tracking or analytics.
